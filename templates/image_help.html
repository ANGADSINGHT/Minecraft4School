How to

Step 1: Open the Windows Apps Menu
Click on the Windows icon at the bottom left (or press the Windows key) to access a list of apps.
Note: if on Windows 11, just search for Powershell with the search bar
Step 2: Scroll to bottom
Scroll to the bottom of the apps page. You should find a folder called Powershell
Step 3: Open Powershell
Click the folder called Powershell, and open the app at the top
Step 4: Paste code in
Once Powershell has fully loaded (give it 10 seconds), paste the code in and hit enter
